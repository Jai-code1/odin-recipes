Chicken Alfredo

Home

Here is a chicken alfredo recipe! In this step by step guide you'll learn how to make a simple savory chicken alfredo that is to die for.

Ingredients

  • 1.25 lbs Chicken breast
  • 12 oz twisted pasta
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp cajun seasoning
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heaby cream
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 oz cream cheese
  • Chooped parsley

Cooking Steps

  1. Boil a pot of salted water. Once pot has reached a boil, add twisted pasta. Cook for about 8-10 minutes. Drain a set pasta aside. Save 1 cup of salty pasta water.
  2. Cut the chicken into even strips and season with cajun, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Heat olive oil in a pan on medium-high heat. Add in chicken and cook for 6-8 minutes. Stirr or flip pieces of chicken occasionally, until golden brown and cooked through. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, on medium heat melt butter and add minced garlic. Saute for 1 minute.
  4. In the same pan, add chicken broth and heavy cream. Scrape the bottom of the pan to loosen flavorful bits stuck there. Lower the heat to medium-low and add in cream cheese, mozzarella, and parmesan. Stir until the cheese has melted and you're left with a creamy sauce. If the sause is to thick add some of the salty pasta water from earlier, a tablespoon at a time.
  5. Add cooked pasta and chicken to the pan. Stir and mix everything together. Let dish simmer for 2 minutes. Enjoy!
