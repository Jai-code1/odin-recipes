Cheesesteak Eggrolls Recipe

Home

Here is a cheesesteak eggroll recipe! In this step by step guide you'll learn how to make a simple savory cheesesteak eggroll.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lbs Thin sliced ribeye steak
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 green bell peper
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder/li>
  • 1 tsp worchestershire sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 6-8 eggroll wrappers
  • 1 egg, beaten (for sealing)

Cooking Steps

  1. Heat olive oil ina a pan on medium-high heat.
  2. Add onion and green pepper, saute for 3-4 minutes until softened.
  3. Add beef, season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and worchestershire sauce.
  4. Cook until just browned, then remove pan from heat.
  5. Stir in Shredded cheese while hot so it melts in the filling.

Egg roll assembly

  1. Lay out a wrapper like a diamond
  2. Place 2-3 tablespoons of filling in the center.
  3. Fold the bottom corner up over the filling, fold in the sides, the roll tightly.
  4. Brush the top corner with beaten egg and seal
  5. Repeat!

Final cooking stage

Air fry at 375 degrees(F) for 10-12 minutes, turning halfway and spraying with oil for crispiness. Serve Hot with ranch!
