Strawberry Frozen Yogurt Bark Recipe

Home

Here is a Strawberry Frozen Yogurt Bark recipe!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups vanilla whole-milk greek yogurt/li>
  • 3 tbsp agave syrup
  • 1/2 green bell peper
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cup sliced strawberries

Steps

  1. Line a baking sheet with parchment. Stir Greek yogurt, agave syrup, and vanilla together in a bowl. Spread the mixture out in an even layer about 1/4 inch thick on the parchment. Scatter strawberries over the top.
  2. Freeze until very firm, at least 4 hours and preferably overnight.
